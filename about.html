About Us

We are a student-run organization with an aim to build an autonomous underwater vehicle to compete at the international RoboSub competition organized by the Association for Unmanned Vehicle Systems Internation (AUVSI), which is held at the Space and Naval Warface Command Research facility in San Diego, California.

The competition draws participants from 50 teams from around the world. All participating teams have to make an autonomous submarine from scratch to perform a variety of visual and acoustic-based challenges designed by competition judges. The course design and tasks are subject to change each year. The competition design and rules are released one month before the competition.

The team is comprised of driven students from diverse backgrounds and disciplines, who aim to explore the applications of cutting-edge technology and engineering in building an autonomous underwater vehicle. As a team, we aim to develop a platform for the exchange of ideas, fostering team spirit and cultivating a strong work ethic.
